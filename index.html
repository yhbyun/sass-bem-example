Hair styling for beginners

Tips and tricks on creating a masterful quiff.

Getting your hair to stand up, and stay up during a busy day in the office can be a bit of a hassle at times. But here's a full-proof method to achieving follicular greatness.

Requirements

  • Hair dryer
  • Styling putty
  • Finishing spray

Method

  1. Wash and condition your hair as normal.
  2. Towel dry you hair until it is only slightly damp.
  3. Rub a small amount of styling putty between the tips of your fingers and style your quiff.
  4. Blow dry into shape, using a brush to pull your hair up.
  5. Add shine and extra hold by applying some finishing spray.
